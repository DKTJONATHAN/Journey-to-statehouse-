How to Register as a Voter:

  1. Visit your nearest IEBC registration center (find at www.iebc.or.ke)
  2. Bring your original ID card/passport and a copy
  3. Fill the registration form with your details
  4. Have your biometrics (fingerprints) taken
  5. Receive your voter's slip with details

Registration is free and takes about 15 minutes!
